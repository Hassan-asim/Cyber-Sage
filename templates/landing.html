Frequently Asked Questions
Your Questions, Answered

Find answers to common questions about Cyber Sage and how it can revolutionize your penetration testing workflow.

What is Cyber Sage?

Cyber Sage is an AI-powered penetration testing platform that helps cybersecurity professionals identify, assess, and report security vulnerabilities with greater speed and precision. It combines automated tools with an expert AI assistant to streamline the entire testing process.

How does the AI work?

Cyber Sage uses a multi-agent AI system. When you describe a potential vulnerability, our AI agents research it, assess its severity using CVSS, provide technical details, and suggest remediation strategies. The CyberSage assistant also provides expert guidance throughout the testing process.

What kind of tests can I perform?

Cyber Sage supports a wide range of tests, including vulnerability assessments, network security scanning (basic, comprehensive, and stealth), automated penetration testing across multiple targets, and advanced vulnerability scanning for web applications and networks.

Is Cyber Sage suitable for both beginners and experts?

Yes! Cyber Sage is designed to be intuitive for beginners while offering the depth and power that experienced penetration testers require. The CyberSage assistant can guide newcomers, while the advanced scanning options and detailed reports provide the necessary tools for experts.
